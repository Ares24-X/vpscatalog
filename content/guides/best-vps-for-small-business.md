## Best VPS for Small Business

Small businesses need reliable hosting with good support. Here are our top picks:

| Provider | Starting Price | Best For | Support |
|----------|---------------|----------|---------|
| DigitalOcean | $12/mo | Growing businesses | Knowledge base, tickets |
| Linode | $12/mo | Beginners | Documentation, community |
| Cloudways | $11/mo | Managed hosting | 24/7 live chat |

### 🥇 DigitalOcean — Best Overall

$12/mo gets you 1 vCPU, 2 GB RAM, and 60 GB storage. The excellent documentation means you can self-serve most issues.

### 🥈 Cloudways — Best Managed Option

If you don't want to manage the server yourself, Cloudways is the best managed VPS for small businesses. Starts at $11/mo and includes 24/7 support.

### 🥉 Linode — Best for Beginners

Linode's documentation and community are unmatched. If someone on your team is learning server management, this is the place to start.
