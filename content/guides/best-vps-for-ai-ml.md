## Best VPS for AI & ML

AI workloads need serious compute. For small to medium projects, cloud VPS with GPU options can be surprisingly affordable.

| Provider | GPU Option | Starting Price | Best For |
|----------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Vultr | NVIDIA A100 | $2.50/mo (CPU) / GPU from $0.69/hr | Budget ML experiments |
| DigitalOcean | Droplet + Paperspace | $6/mo (CPU) | Development & prototyping |
| Hetzner | Dedicated GPU | €3.99/mo (CPU) / GPU from €89/mo | Serious training on a budget |

### By Use Case

| Use Case | Recommended | Why |
|----------|------------|-----|
| Experimenting / learning | Vultr GPU ($0.69/hr) | Pay by the hour, cheap to try |
| Running inference (small models) | DigitalOcean $12/mo | Enough for lightweight AI |
| Training models | Hetzner GPU (€89/mo) | Best price for dedicated GPU |
| Large-scale training | Dedicated Hetzner | Full control, high performance |

### Quick Tip

For AI work, don't get locked annual contracts. GPU pricing drops every year. Stick with hourly billing (Vultr) or month-to-month (Hetzner) so you can upgrade when newer GPUs launch.
