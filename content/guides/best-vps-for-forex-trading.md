### What Forex Traders Need

- **Low latency** — Every millisecond matters in scalping
- **Proximity to broker** — Choose a data center near your broker's servers
- **99.99% uptime** — Don't miss trades due to downtime
- **Static IP** — Some brokers require whitelisted IPs
- **Windows VPS** — Many trading platforms (MT4/MT5) need Windows
