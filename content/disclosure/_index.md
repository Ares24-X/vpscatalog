## Affiliate Disclosure

VPS Catalog is committed to providing honest, independent reviews of VPS hosting providers. To support the operation of this site, we participate in affiliate marketing programs.

**What this means:** Some of the links on this site are affiliate links. If you click on one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

## Our Promise

- We **only** recommend providers we've personally tested
- Affiliate relationships **never** affect our ratings or rankings
- We clearly mark affiliate links where appropriate
- If a provider offers a poor experience, we say so — regardless of affiliate status
- We do **not** accept payment for positive reviews

## How We Choose What to Review

Providers are selected based on market relevance and reader interest, not affiliate commissions. We regularly update our reviews to reflect current pricing and performance.
