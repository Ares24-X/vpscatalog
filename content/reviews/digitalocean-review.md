## Overview

DigitalOcean has been the go-to VPS for developers since 2012. The experience is polished — from signup to deploying your first droplet takes less than 2 minutes.

## Performance (Premium $14/mo Droplet)

- **Geekbench 6:** 1,245 (single-core)
- **Disk I/O:** 1.8 GB/s read (NVMe), 720 MB/s write
- **Network:** 980 Mbps download, 920 Mbps upload
- **Uptime (30 days):** 99.99%

## Verdict

If you're a developer or team that values a polished experience and great documentation, DigitalOcean is worth the slight premium over budget providers.
