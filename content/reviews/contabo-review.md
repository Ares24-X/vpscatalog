## Overview

Contabo is the spec king. At €5.99/mo you get 4 vCPUs and 8 GB RAM — competitors charge $12-24/mo for similar specs. The trade-off is network consistency.

## Performance (€5.99/mo Plan)

- **Geekbench 6:** 1,432 (single-core)
- **Disk I/O:** 1.4 GB/s read, 620 MB/s write
- **Network:** High variance (peak 800 Mbps, off-peak 300 Mbps)
- **Uptime (30 days):** 99.92%

## Verdict

Contabo is a great pick if you need raw CPU and RAM on a tight budget. The network inconsistency means it's not ideal for latency-sensitive applications.
