## Overview

Cloudways isn't a hosting provider — it's a **managed platform** that runs on top of DigitalOcean, Vultr, Linode, AWS, and Google Cloud. You pay for the underlying server + Cloudways' management fee.

## Verdict

Cloudways is excellent if you want managed VPS without learning server administration. The 24/7 support actually knows what they're doing.

For developers comfortable with the command line, going direct with DigitalOcean or Vultr saves you 45-50% with equivalent performance.
