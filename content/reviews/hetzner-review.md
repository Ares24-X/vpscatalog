## Overview

Hetzner is a German hosting company that's been around since 1997. Their cloud VPS line launched a few years ago and immediately shook up the market with aggressive pricing.

## Performance (CX32 — 2 vCPU, 8 GB, €6.99/mo)

- **Geekbench 6:** 1,856 (single-core)
- **Disk I/O:** 2.1 GB/s read (NVMe), 870 MB/s write
- **Network:** 1.8 Gbps download, 1.2 Gbps upload
- **Uptime (30 days):** 99.97%

The CX32 outperforms most $20/mo plans from other providers.

## Verdict

If performance is your priority and you don't need a fancy control panel, Hetzner is unmatched value. Best for users comfortable with the command line.
