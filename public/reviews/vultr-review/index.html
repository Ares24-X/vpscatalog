Vultr Review 2026

★ 4.5/5 Visit Provider →

✅ Pros

  • 32 global data centers — most of any VPS provider
  • Prices start at just $2.50/mo
  • Hourly billing, perfect for short-term projects
  • Bare metal and GPU instances available
  • One-click apps for WordPress, Docker, LEMP, etc.

❌ Cons

  • Support can be slow on lower-tier plans
  • Control panel interface is functional but dated
  • No managed database offering (unlike DigitalOcean)

Overview

Vultr is one of the most popular VPS providers, and for good reason. With 32 data centers worldwide and prices starting at $2.50/mo, it’s a strong contender for budget-conscious users who need global reach.

Performance

I ran a series of benchmarks on Vultr’s $6/mo plan (1 vCPU, 1 GB RAM, 25 GB SSD):

  • Geekbench 6: 1,012 (single-core)
  • Disk I/O: 1.2 GB/s read, 580 MB/s write
  • Network: 950 Mbps download, 850 Mbps upload (US East)
  • Uptime (30 days): 99.98%
