Vultr wins on entry price and offers more storage at the mid-tier. Linode offers more RAM at higher tiers.

Choose Linode if:

  • You’re a beginner and need excellent documentation
  • You want a clean, simple dashboard
  • You value StackScripts for automated setup

Choose Vultr if:

  • You need more global data centers (32 vs 11)
  • Budget is your primary concern
  • You need hourly billing or GPU instances
  • You want the $2.50 entry plan
